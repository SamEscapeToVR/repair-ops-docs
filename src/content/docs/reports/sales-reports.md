# Sales Reports

RepairOps provides sales reports at multiple time scales — from custom date ranges to monthly, quarterly, and yearly summaries. All sales reports combine revenue from repair invoices and POS transactions automatically.

---

## Day Range Sales Report

The most flexible sales report. Pick any start and end date to see revenue trends across the range.

### Running the Report

1. Navigate to **Reports → Day Range Sales** in the sidebar.
2. Select your **shop**.
3. Set the **start date** and **end date**.
4. Click **Generate Report**.

### What's Included

**Summary Row** at the top:

- Total revenue, total tax, transaction count, daily average revenue, and highest/lowest revenue days within the range.

**Daily Breakdown Table** showing one row per day:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Date** | Calendar date |
| **Transactions** | Number of completed sales |
| **Labor** | Labor revenue |
| **Parts** | Parts revenue |
| **Tax** | Tax collected |
| **Total** | Revenue including tax |

**Trend Indicators** — The report highlights days that are significantly above or below your daily average, making it easy to spot patterns like slow Mondays or busy Saturdays.

## Periodic Sales Reports

For standard accounting periods, use the preset periodic reports. These automatically calculate date ranges and include prior-period comparison.

### Monthly Sales Report

Shows revenue for a selected month with comparison to the previous month.

1. Navigate to **Reports → Monthly Sales**.
2. Select your **shop** and **month/year**.
3. Click **Generate Report**.

The report includes: total revenue, labor/parts split, tax collected, transaction count, and a percentage change column comparing to the prior month (e.g., "+12% vs. last month").

### Quarterly Sales Report

Shows revenue for a selected quarter (Q1–Q4) with comparison to the same quarter last year.

1. Navigate to **Reports → Quarterly Sales**.
2. Select your **shop**, **quarter**, and **year**.
3. Click **Generate Report**.

Includes monthly subtotals within the quarter and a year-over-year comparison. This is especially useful for seasonal businesses that want to compare Q4 holiday traffic year over year.

### Yearly Sales Report

Shows revenue for an entire calendar year with monthly breakdown.

1. Navigate to **Reports → Yearly Sales**.
2. Select your **shop** and **year**.
3. Click **Generate Report**.

Includes: 12 monthly rows, quarterly subtotals, annual total, and comparison to the prior year. The yearly report also shows your busiest and slowest months.

---

## Organization Roll-Up (Pro & Enterprise)

If you operate multiple shops, Monthly, Quarterly, and Yearly reports offer an **Organization Roll-Up** option. Toggle this on to see combined revenue across all your locations in a single report, with per-shop subtotals.

The roll-up uses each shop's local currency and tax settings — it does not attempt to convert currencies or normalize tax rates. Each shop's section shows its own tax details.

---

## Prior-Period Comparison

All periodic reports include automatic prior-period comparison:

| Report | Compared Against |
|--------|-----------------|
| Monthly | Previous month |
| Quarterly | Same quarter, previous year |
| Yearly | Previous year |

Comparison columns show both absolute change (e.g., "+$2,340") and percentage change (e.g., "+8.5%"). Positive changes are highlighted in green, negative in red.
