RepairOps provides a comprehensive suite of tools for repair shop management across all service tiers. This section covers all features available on Starter, Pro, and Enterprise plans.

## Core Features <TierBadge tier="starter" />

### [Knowledge Base + KB Chat](/features/knowledge-base/)
Build an internal knowledge base with AI-powered search. Create rich-text articles with attachments, manage publishing workflows, and enable your team to instantly find answers via RAG-powered chat.

### [System Builder](/features/system-builder/)
Design and recommend custom computer systems with AI assistance. Access 31K+ components from BuildCores, enable compatibility checking, and share curated builds with customers through the portal.

## Advanced Features <TierBadge tier="pro" />

### [AI Gateway](/features/ai-gateway/)
Integrate AI across your workflow with flexible provider support. Choose between bring-your-own-API-key or RepairOps-managed credits. Power intake parsing, diagnostics, voice-to-text, QC checks, and more.

### [POS Terminal](/features/pos-terminal/)
Process non-repair sales directly in RepairOps. Manage cash drawers, process multiple payment methods, and generate daily reconciliation reports.

### [Analytics & KPI Dashboards](/features/analytics/)
Track shop performance with real-time dashboards. Monitor revenue, throughput, turnaround time, QC pass rates, and tech-level performance. Pro tier unlocks AI insights and PDF reports.

### [Plugin System](/features/plugins/)
Extend RepairOps with free and premium plugins. Connect label printers, booking systems, CRM integrations, and more. Pro users access advanced plugins like Google Reviews, Time Clock, and Customer Export.

### [iFixit Repair Guides](/features/ifixit-guides/)
Embed step-by-step repair guides from iFixit directly in your workflow. Search millions of guides, attach them to tickets, and let AI suggest relevant guides automatically.

## Enterprise Features <TierBadge tier="enterprise" />

Enterprise-only capabilities include white-label branding, REST API access, SSO/SAML authentication, and self-hosted AI via Ollama. See [Admin Documentation](/admin/) for configuration details.
