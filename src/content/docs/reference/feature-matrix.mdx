RepairOps offers three base tiers — <TierBadge tier="starter" />, <TierBadge tier="pro" />, and <TierBadge tier="enterprise" /> — plus separate AI add-ons. This page summarizes current pricing, hard limits, feature gates, and AI packaging.

## Plan Overview

| | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|---|---------|-----|------------|
| **Monthly Price** | $49 | $99 | $199 |
| **Annual Price** | $470/yr | $950/yr | $1,910/yr |
| **Best For** | Single-shop teams | Growing multi-shop ops | Franchises and compliance-heavy orgs |
| **Trial** | 14 days + 50 AI credits | 14 days + 50 AI credits | Sales-assisted onboarding |

Starter and Pro are self-serve. Enterprise onboarding is typically sales-assisted, but the same platform capabilities apply once provisioned.

## AI Add-Ons

| Add-On | Monthly Cost | Managed Credits | Capabilities |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|--------------|
| Disabled | $0 | 0 | No AI features |
| AI Ready | $79 | 0 | BYOK-only access to supported AI tasks |
| AI Assist | $79 | 200 | Intake parse, note cleanup, customer summary, KB search |
| AI Copilot | $149 | 500 | Full managed AI task coverage |
| Enterprise AI | Included with Enterprise | Unlimited | BYOK + managed + Ollama + fallback + audit |

**AI Ready** and **AI Assist** cost the same, but they solve different problems. AI Ready is for BYOK teams. AI Assist is for teams that want managed usage and a predictable monthly credit pool.

## Credit Packs

Need more credits beyond your monthly allocation? Purchase credit packs at any time.

| Pack | Credits | Price | Cost Per Credit |
|------|---------|-------|-----------------|
| Small | 100 | $15 | $0.15 |
| Medium | 500 | $60 | $0.12 |
| Large | 1,000 | $100 | $0.10 |

Purchased credits are non-refundable and expire after 12 months of inactivity. Monthly included credits do not roll over.

## Choosing the Right Plan

**<TierBadge tier="starter" /> Starter** is ideal for single-location shops with a small team. It includes the full repair workflow, Knowledge Base, iFixit guides, and manual System Builder workflows.

**<TierBadge tier="pro" /> Pro** is designed for growing shops that need multiple locations, advanced analytics, the Plugin Marketplace, and the full AI Gateway control surface.

**<TierBadge tier="enterprise" /> Enterprise** is built for organizations that require white-label branding, SSO, API access, self-hosted AI, and audit-ready controls.
