## Tips

**Discovering shortcuts** — Press **?** on any page to see a quick-reference overlay of shortcuts available in that context.

**Search is powerful** — The **Cmd/Ctrl + K** search accepts ticket numbers (T001234), customer names, device identifiers, and status keywords. Results appear instantly as you type.

**Chaining actions** — On the Kanban board, use arrow keys to select a ticket, press **Enter** to open it, then **T** to immediately transition its status. This three-keystroke flow replaces multiple clicks.
