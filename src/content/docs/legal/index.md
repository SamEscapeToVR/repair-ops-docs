## Quick Facts

| Topic | Summary |
|-------|---------|
| **Data ownership** | Your Organization owns all Customer Data. We never sell it. |
| **Encryption** | AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit, envelope encryption for secrets |
| **Tenant isolation** | Row-Level Security (RLS) at the database level |
| **Advertising cookies** | None. Zero. We don't use ad trackers. |
| **AI data usage** | AI providers are contractually prohibited from training on your data |
| **Data deletion** | 30-day read-only after cancellation, then permanent deletion within 90 days |
| **GDPR compliance** | Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers |
| **CCPA compliance** | RepairOps is a "Service Provider" — we never sell or share personal information |
